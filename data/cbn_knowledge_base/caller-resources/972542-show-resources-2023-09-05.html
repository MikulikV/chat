Antenna Information 

Agent Note

Sub channels are configured with a dot (e.g. 10.4, 10.8, etc.) and float between standard channels. Local channels like PBS and the CBN News Channel are meant to be free broadcast channels when you access them using a digital antenna.

A digital antenna is easy to install and typically takes less than five minutes. Antennas can be found at national retailers like Best Buy or Wal-Mart.

For those who aren't sure their antenna's are installed correctly, please offer to send one or more of the following:

  • ENCV - CBN News Channel Training Video (Electronic Link) 
  • ENCB - CBN News Channel Instruction Fact Sheet (Download Link)
  • NCGB - CBN News Channel Instruction Booklet (Print)

FAQs

How Can I Find Out What Is Playing This Month? 

Please visit the Show page to find out more about our various news programs: cbn.com/cbnnews/all-shows 

You can also sign up for our alerts so you may receive updates: cbn.com/cbnnews/email-forms 

I'm Not Seeing Channel Listings. What Do I Do? 

We can’t always control the channel listings but will do our best to work with the cable providers. In the meantime, check out our current listings for CBN: cbn.com/newschannel/schedule 

Can I Watch CBN With Closed Captioning?

Every CBN channel provides closed captioning, and we are constantly ensuring that we are in compliance with FCC regulations.

What Do I Do If My Picture Is Pixelating Or Skipping? 

  • First, try moving around the antenna, if you have one. This will often improve the picture. 
  • Second, try restarting it (unplug it from the wall and plug it back in).
  • Third, rescan your channel.

Agent Note: You may offer to send ENCV.

Can I Suggest Programming? 

Yes, we value your feedback. If there is a topic you would like to see shown on the CBN News Channel, we'll happily pass your suggestion on to our programming team as viewer input.

Agent Note: Submit a Feedback Referral.

Do I Have to Pay for Programming? 

No. Programming received through a digital antenna is free, as well as watching online for free at cbn.com/700club 

When Do I Need to Run a Channel Scan?

Run a channel scan after setting up the antenna. If you already have an over-the-air antenna and have not recently run a scan, you may need to rescan your channels as CBN Channel local stations may have been recently added to your area.

Where Should I Place My Antenna?

To minimize interference and improve your signal reception, position the antenna near a window.
