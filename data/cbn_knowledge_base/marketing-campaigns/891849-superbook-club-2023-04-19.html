Charge Date Questions

When Will I Be Charged?

You will be charged $25 immediately and you will receive the latest Superbook DVD release.
Thereafter, you will be charged $25 every 4 - 6 weeks (the last week of each month) when a new DVD release becomes available.

Agent Note: If the caller joins 20 days before the 26th charge date, their card will not be charged again for that month.

Can I Pick a 
Different Charge Date?

If a caller requests an alternate charge date, please process these calls as follows:

Category: Account Services
Project: Pledge Problems/Changes. 

Agent Note: Select Superbook Pledge Problems/Changes from the introduction page and submit the referral. The Superbook Partner Assistance team will handle their request. 

Can I Delay My First Payment?

Yes.

Process the initial donation. In Pledge Special Instructions, indicate the caller's request for the day to charge their account.

Substitution/Opt-Out Questions 

Can I Join the CBN Animation Club 
and Get Streaming Only (No DVDs)?

Yes.

Use the CBN Animation Club project for joining the club and then use the Left Navigation Bar to submit a referral detailing the caller's request for no DVDs.

Can I Choose or Substitute DVDS?

No. The caller cannot choose or substitute DVDs.

As a CBN Animation Club Member, Can I Opt-Out 
of Receiving Explorer or GizmoGO! DVDs?

Yes. You can opt out of Superbook Explorer or GizmoGO! and only receive new Superbook episodes. If you opt out, CBN will only charge your account for the months that a new Superbook Episode releases.

If a member decides to opt out, process these calls as follows:  

Category: Account Services
Project: Pledge Problems/Changes

Agent Note: Select Change Superbook Participation option in the script and complete the referral form.

Can I Join the CBN Animation Club By Mail?

No. CBN Animation Club membership is available through credit/debit card and EFT only.

However, the caller can mail in a check for each individual Superbook DVD when it becomes available.

Address on Envelope:

CBN
CBN Center
Virginia Beach VA 23463

Payable to: CBN

Be sure to include the Superbook DVD title in the memo portion of your check. Also, be sure to indicate the number of DVDs you wish to order. 

Agent Note: Mail-in donations are only accepted for individual DVD ordering.

Delivery/Multiple Address Questions

I Want a Copy of the DVD for Each 
of My Grandchildren. How Can I Do That?

2 options:

  1. Join the CBN Animation Club and have the 3 DVDs delivered to your address. Once you receive the DVDs, you can mail a DVD to each of your grandchildren.  
  2. Create a CBN Animation Club Membership for each of your grandchildren and have the DVDs shipped to their address.  

Can I Have the Extra Wallet 
Copies Shipped to Another Address?

We are unable to split up the package and deliver the extra copies to separate addresses.  

The 2 extra copies are packaged together as a single package. However, extra copies are in a wallet cover made from heavy cardstock, so they are flat. This makes them easy and inexpensive for you to mail.

Can I Have my DVDs 
Shipped to a Different Address? 

Yes. You may ship your DVDs to an alternative address. Please note - all DVD’s will be sent to the same address.  

Category: Product
Project: CBN Animation Club 

Agent Note: Be sure the check SHIP TO ADDRESS INFO on the Address Response Page and submit the alternative shipping address.  

If an existing CBN Animation Club Member would like to change their ship-to address, process the call as follows: 

Category: Account Services
Project: Partner Records/Status Changes*

Agent Note: Select the Caller Needs to Update Partner Record/Repeated for Address Change option. Submit a referral capturing the old ship-to address and the new address. 

What is the Shipping Speed? 
Can I Expedite the Shipping?

You will receive your DVD within 2 – 3 weeks of each charge. There will be approximately 4 weeks between DVD releases. We do not offer expedited shipping at this time.

How Many Languages is Superbook Translated? 

All Superbook DVDs received by CBN Animation Club membership have menu selections for both English and Spanish, audio and subtitles.

Our goal is to broadcast the episodes in 50 or more languages including English.

Our foreign language episodes are created for special Superbook broadcasts in those countries and are not available to the public at this time.

What Superbook Academy Content Do I Have Access to With My Membership?

CBN Animation Club members can access exclusive Superbook content in the CBN Family App and the Superbook Kids Bible App. CBN Animation Club members have access to the following: 

  • Superbook Academy Signpost Videos 
  • Superbook Academy Bible Background Videos 
  • Family Discussion Guide (PDF)
  • Episode Activity Book

Am I Automatically a CBN Animation Club Member if I’m a CBN Monthly Partner?

We offer a separate membership program for CBN Animation. When you become a CBN Animation Club member, all funds go directly to the mission of CBN Animation.

How Can I Share Superbook with Others?

If the user does not already have an account, they will need to create one first using this link.

As part of the registration process, the user will be provided with options to share Superbook with their friends.

Users can access the sharing screen from "settings" on the Superbook website.

When a friend uses the sharing link provided to them, it will result in awarding Superpoints and unlocking episodes for the user who shared the link.
