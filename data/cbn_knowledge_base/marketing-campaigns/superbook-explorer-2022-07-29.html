Superbook Explorer

Marketing Article

Written by Eric Howell | Last published at: July 29, 2022

Description 

Superbook Explorer is an interactive DVD created for children to take a deeper look into Bible stories.

Children will explore amazing archaeological and historical aspects of the Bible, and they will gain a firm foundation of faith that will enable them to confidently share what they have learned with others.

As a Superbook Club Member, can I opt out of receiving Superbook Explorer DVDs?

Yes. If a caller would like to opt-out of receiving Explorer DVDs, their account will not be charged when the monthly release is and Explorer DVD.

If a member wishes to opt-out, please process these calls as follows:

Category: Account Services

Project: Pledge Problems/Changes

Select Superbook and complete the form. Be sure to capture specifically that the caller would like to opt-out of Explorer DVDs.

As a Superbook Club Member, when will I receive Superbook Explorer DVDs? 

You will receive Superbook Explorer episodes occasionally throughout the year as your monthly Superbook release.
