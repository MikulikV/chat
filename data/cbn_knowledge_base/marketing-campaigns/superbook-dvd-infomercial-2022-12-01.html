Superbook DVD Infomercial

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: December 01, 2022

Description 

The Superbook Infomercial is a 26 minute video that highlights the ways in which Superbook enriches the lives of children.

The infomercial is aired on several broadcasting networks and callers are prompted to call 1-800-853-8314, to receive special Superbook offers.

FAQ's

Can I choose/substitute the DVDs in my order?

No. The caller cannot choose or substitute DVDs.

With my order, will I become a CBN Animation Club member?

No. The caller will not become a CBN Animation Club member with their order. 

Will I receive my order by Christmas?

While the effort is made to ship orders as soon as possible, we cannot guarantee delivery by Christmas.
