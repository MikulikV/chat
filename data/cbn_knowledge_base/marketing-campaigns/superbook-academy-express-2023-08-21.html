What is Each Episode About?

Superbook Academy Express wraps a full length episode into one video, promoted as an "at-home Sunday School lesson presented by Gizmo." The popular "Chris & Joy dilemma" are back, which help teach children lessons from Bible stories, such as loving others in "The Good Samaritan."

What Age are SB Academy Express Geared Towards?

Grade school ages.

How is SB Academy Express Different than Other Series?

The new addition to this series is the Family Resource Guide! This is a physical booklet with Bible lessons and activities that will be included with the DVD. It is exclusive to the SB Academy Express series.

What is the Family Resource Guide?

Unique to the SB Academy Express series, the Family Resource Guide is a booklet that brings the family together for sharing the Bible with children in a way they can understand and enjoy!

  • It is a physical booklet of 16 pages, which will be mailed with each month's DVD release (only when that particular month is a SB Academy Express episode).
  • Includes:
    • Application lesson of the Bible story (in the episode)
    • Coloring pages
    • Activity pages
    • and more (which may vary)
  • Dimensions:
    • 5.5 x 8.5 inches

It can also be downloaded from the Superbook website or CBN Family once the DVD has been released, typically the 26th of every month. Only Club members will be able to access the downloadable version or SB Academy Express streaming.

Delete

How is SB Academy Express different from SB Academy?

SB Academy Express is a condensed version of SB Academy, just like the name implies! 

SB Academy is designed for churches or larger groups, it includes:

  • Both a full length and short version of a SB episode
  • 3 Lessons that include Bible teaching
  • Scripture memorization
  • Activities 
  • Games
  • Teacher notes

SB Academy Express is designed for families, which includes the typical package that other series includes, such as:

  • Family Discussion Guide (pamphlet-style, not booklet)
  • Trading Cards
  • Salvation prayer/poem

But additionally includes:

  • Family Resource Guide (5.5 x 8.5 booklet)
    • Bible Q & A
    • Application lessons from the Bible story
    • Coloring pages
    • Activity pages
    • and more (which may vary)

SB Academy Express includes "some" lessons and activities from SB Academy, but not as many resources as a larger group may need, such as Sunday School classes.
