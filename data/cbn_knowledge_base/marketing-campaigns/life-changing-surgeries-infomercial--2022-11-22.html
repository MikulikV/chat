What Are The Common Types of Surgeries That You Sponsor? 

Some of our surgeries include:

  •  Appendectomy  
  • Ear Surgery 
  • Burn Treatments  
  • Goiter
  • Cataracts
  • Hernia
  • Clubfoot
  • Intestinal Obstruction 
  • Cleft Lip/Palate
  • Obstetric Fistula  
  • Congenital heart defects
  • Cyst Removal   

Can I Designate My Donation To a Specific Individual?

Yes, in our Life-Changing Surgeries catalog, you can read the stories of those waiting for relief and donate to the specific individuals listed online.

To view the catalog click here

How Can I Request to Sponsor Myself or Someone I Know? 

Most LCS candidates are referred to by a local hospital or health center that is aware of the LCS program or discover at one of our medical brigades or an OB-trained Community Health Volunteer. 

To request for you or someone you know to be sponsored for a Life-Changing Surgery, you must connect with the local LCS coordinator in your country.
