What age is the series geared towards?

The series is intended for the 7–12 age range, although some episodes will be suitable for younger ages. Due to some of the dramatic elements, ratings will be made available on any DVD that we feel should be screened by a parent.

How can I watch a free Superbook episode?

Download the free Superbook Kids Bible App from your smartphone, tablet, Kindle, or Smart TV. The app features free, full-length episodes from Seasons 1-3. 

Why are some Superbook episodes available on Amazon but not all?

Seasons 1 – 3 of the Superbook series are available to stream for free on Amazon. Currently, there are no plans to release seasons 4 – 5 however, they are available for CBN Animation Club members on the CBN Family App and Superbook Kids Bible App.

Charisma Publishing has a contract with CBN to purchase Superbook DVDs to sell in retail outlets. These DVDs may then be sold online (Amazon) or in physical stores.

Note: It is also possible that some DVDs are being resold from CBN donors/CBN Animation Club members via Amazon, eBay, etc. Callers may purchase from Amazon however, CBN encourages callers to buy at their own discretion. CBN is not responsible for any damaged items sold by a third party seller.
