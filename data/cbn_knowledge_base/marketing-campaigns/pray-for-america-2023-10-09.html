Pray for America

Marketing Article

Written by Tara Jackson | Last published at: October 09, 2023

Description 

Pray for America is a 40-day prayer campaign dedicated to consecration, prayer, and repentance as our nation approaches the U.S. presidential election. 



Briefing Notes

Update: 9/15/23 

New Marketing Campaign - Pray for America 2023 (All Agents)

Beginning 9/18/23, the Pray for America packet will be offered on the broadcast. Agentless ordering will be activated for this product. 

As a reminder, there are a few scenarios where the IVR cannot process the caller’s order and will route to an agent:

  • Phone not recognized
  • Address not shippable
  • Product Already Ordered

When you receive a call that the IVR cannot process, there will be an auto-script pop. You will then proceed to verify the caller’s account and order the product as usual. 

Release Date: 9/7/23

New Marketing Campaign - Pray for America 2023 (All Agents)  

From 9/18/23 - 10/27/23, CBN is asking believers to join us as we Pray for America for 40 days. These 40 days will be a time of consecration, prayer, and repentance as we ask the Lord for unity and revival in our nation. 

Viewers can commit to Pray for America and receive a special prayer packet by visiting PrayForAmerica.com or calling 1-800-700-7000. Participants will be added to an interactive map of America, representing a point of prayer in their town/city.

The website will be live closer to the launch date.
