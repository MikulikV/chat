Description 

The CBN Animation Superbook Giving-Cause is drawing new and lapsed audiences to a new giving website to support the mission of sharing the Gospel with children in their own language.

New Marketing Campaign – CBN Animation Superbook Giving-Cause/Share The Gospel Website (T2)

  • Successful Families: Power in the Love of Family (downloadable resource)
  • Complete Box Set of Season One
  • Instant streaming of Superbook content in CBN Family

FAQ's

Is this Part of the CBN Animation Club?

No, this Marketing Campaign is separate from the CBN Animation Club. However, as the name implies, the donations received help support the CBN Animation/Superbook brand.

Are Monthly DVDs Included?

No monthly DVDs are included as with CBN Animation Club membership. 

Will the Donors Receive Any Benefits in Giving?

Yes! Donors who give a donation of any dollar amount ($1 or more) one-time or monthly will automatically receive:

  • Successful Families: Power in the Love of Family (downloadable resource)
  • Completely Box Set of Season One 
  • Instant Streaming of Superbook on CBN Family

How Long will One-Time Givers Have Streaming Access?

Donors who give a one-time gift will have streaming access to the Superbook episodes in CBN Family for one month.
