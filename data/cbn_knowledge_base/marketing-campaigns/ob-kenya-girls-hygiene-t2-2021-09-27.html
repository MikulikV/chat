International Day of the Girl

Description 

Operation Blessing is accepting donations towards helping girls in Kenya receive the hygiene supplies they need to stay in school. 


Briefing Notes

Beginning 9/1/21, Operation Blessing will distribute hygiene packs to girls from ages 12-18 in marginalized areas with high poverty. During the distribution, the girls will attend a hygiene training where they will be taught personal hygiene, the importance of education, and stress management.  


This campaign is in honor of International Day of the Girl, which is on 10/11/21.

FAQ's

How Does My Donation Help?

Girls who cannot afford feminine hygiene products are vulnerable to severe health risks and infections because they often resort to unhygienic solutions.

With your help, Operation Blessing distributes feminine hygiene products to girls, accompanied by hygiene lessons done in partnership with community leaders and local churches. This program aims to boost girls' confidence and freedom to attend school without worrying about being mocked or criticized each month.

Operation Blessing also simultaneously runs a program to raise their male peers’ awareness and understanding of feminine health.

How Many Girls Will I Help?

Approximately 2,000 girls will receive a one-year supply of sanitary pads. 

During the distribution, the girls will attend a hygiene training where they will be taught personal hygiene, the importance of education, and stress management.
