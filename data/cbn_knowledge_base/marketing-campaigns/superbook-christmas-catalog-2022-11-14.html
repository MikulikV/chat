Superbook Christmas Catalog

Marketing Article

Written by Tara Jackson | Last published at: November 14, 2022

Description 

In appreciation of our CBN Animation Club members, during the holiday season, Club members will receive special access to our Superbook Christmas Catalog. This catalog offers Superbook products at discounted donation amounts. 



Briefing Notes

Release Date: 11/10/22

New Marketing Campaign – 2022 Superbook Christmas Catalog (T2)  

Starting 11/11/22, CBN Animation Club members (and former Club members) who have an email address on file will receive an email about our 2022 Christmas Catalog. This catalog will offer Seasons 1 – 5 of Superbook, GizmoGO!, Superbook Storybooks, and Superbook Songs CD/CD for discounted donation amounts. For fastest delivery, members can order at CBN.com/SuperbookSpecialOffer. 

At the end of the month, CBN Animation Club members will also receive a physical Christmas Catalog and an order form to mail back their orders. The Superbook Christmas Catalog offer ends 12/31.

For call processing for members who want to order via phone, click the accordion labeled "Call 1-800-700-7000."

For Canada:

Category: Current Promotions
Project: Superbook Christmas Catalog
Agent Note: When entering the project, select the option: Order from Canada Catalog. 

This will bring up an internet form where you will check off the products that the caller wishes to order. Callers may only order 1 of each product.

You will be prompted to collect the payment information for both current and lapsed club members. (Do not submit a referral to use payment on file.)

If you experience technical difficulties, follow the steps below to manually fix the donation form:

  1. Click the settings icon in the upper-right corner of your screen.
  2. Select Open with Microsoft Edge.

This will open the donation form in a new Edge browser and allow information to be entered into the fields.
