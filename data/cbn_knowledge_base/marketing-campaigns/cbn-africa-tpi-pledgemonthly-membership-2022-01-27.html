FAQ's

How Does the Show TPi Support the Mission of CBN Africa?

TPi is not only a broadcast but a part of the ministry, CBN Africa. The support from loving partners like you is making it possible for TPi to provide viewers with a positive and life-changing television show and tangibly help local communities across Africa.

By joining with CBN Africa/TPi, your donations do the following:

  • Gives urgent aid to at-risk children, families, and individuals by partnering with CBN's disaster relief, medical missions, and freshwater projects across the continent. 
  • Offers life-changing surgeries, clean drinking water, and prayer support to those in need.
  • Brings the gospel to children by providing opportunities for orphans and vulnerable children through Orphan's Promise. 
  • Airs Superbook, CBN's animated TV series, across Africa to teach children about Jesus, biblical truths, and life lessons through Bible-based adventures and encourages them to turn their lives over to God. 

Am I automatically a CBN Africa Partner if I'm a CBN Monthly Partner?

We actually offer a separate membership program for CBN Africa/TPi. When you become a CBN Africa/TPi Monthly partner, all funds go directly to the mission of CBN Africa. 

Agent Note: Please share the following statement with the CBN Partners as needed. 

“Approximately 2.69% of all CBN giving goes toward supporting the work of CBN Africa programs. CBN partners make a substantial contribution toward our initiatives in Africa.”

Can Canadian Donors Give Towards CBN Africa/TPi?

Yes! However, since CBN Africa/TPi is a U.S. organization, all donations will be processed in U.S. dollars and donors will not receive a Canadian tax-deductible receipt.
