How long was Dede Robertson with CBN?

In 1960, Pat founded The Christian Broadcasting Network and purchased a defunct UHF-TV station, which he renovated and renamed WYAH.  CBN began broadcasting on October 1, 1961. Its flagship program, The 700 Club, was created in 1966, and has become one of the longest-running television programs. Always at Pat’s side, Dede Robertson was an integral part of the birth and growth of The Christian Broadcasting Network.

What was Dede's role at CBN?

Dede Robertson served as a founding member of the boards and continued valued member of the boards of the Christian Broadcasting Network, Inc., Regent University, and Operation Blessing International Relief and Development Corporation.
