OB Kenya Drought

Marketing Article

Written by Tara Jackson | Last published at: June 13, 2022

Description 

This summer, Operation Blessing is looking for 500 people to partner alongside us to provide a least one month's worth of food for Kenyan families in need due to severe drought. 



Briefing Notes

Release Date: 6/13/22

New Marketing Campaign - OB Kenya Drought (T2)

From 6/15/22 - 7/31/22, Operation Blessing is raising donations to help feed drought victims in Kenya. 


Highlights

  • Other Info: 
  • Nomads in Kenya are starving due to severe drought. Although they have access to small amounts of drinking water, the earth is parched and barren. They have lost their livestock and their crops. 
  • For only $45, donors provide a family of 5 in Kenya with a food packet that can last them a whole month.
