Superbook Merry Christmas Box

Marketing Article

Written by Glena Fitzmorris | Last published at: November 18, 2022

Description 

Superbook and Helping the Home Front are teaming up with generous donors to help bring holiday cheer to active-duty military families.  

For a gift of $25 or more, donors can send a special Christmas gift box to children in an active military family. 



Briefing Notes

Release Date: 11/10/22

Marketing Campaign – Superbook Merry Christmas Box (T2) 

Beginning 11/11/22, Superbook and Helping the Home Front are teaming up with generous donors to help bring holiday cheer to active-duty military families.  

For a gift of $25 or more, donors can send a special Christmas gift box to children in an active military family. 


Highlights

  • Related Brand
    • Superbook 
  • Related Program
    • Helping the Home Front
  • Other Info: 
  • Each box is $25
  • Military chaplains will facilitate the distribution of these Christmas boxes to qualifying families.
  • The box has wonderful surprises for children ages 3-12 years old.
  • For every new membership to the CBN Animation Club, a Superbook Merry Christmas box will be sponsored.
  • Start Date: 11/11/22
  • End Date: 12/24/22

FAQ's

Can I order a box for my own active-military family?

Donors are unable to order Christmas boxes for themselves. 

Agent Note: Do NOT submit a referral for these requests.

Can I request a box for a certain active-military family? 

Donors are unable to designate where boxes are distributed. 

Agent Note: Do NOT submit a referral for these requests.

Where are the boxes going to be distributed?

US only
