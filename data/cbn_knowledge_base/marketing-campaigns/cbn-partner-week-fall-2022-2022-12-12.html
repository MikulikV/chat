FAQ's

Why Does CBN No Longer Promote Telethon?

"Since May 2020, we have changed these campaigns to closely resemble our regular programming. The programming airs additional segments which highlight the impact our partners make through their giving and encourage viewers to join us in our work to spread the Gospel and help the poor and disadvantaged."   

Note: The term Partner Week is being used for internal use within the ministry only. These terms will not appear on the broadcast or marketing materials. Do not use this term when communicating with callers. Please refer to this time as our normal broadcast to callers.     

Since May 2020, in order to abide by broadcast guidelines and remain sensitive to the pandemic, the term "Telethon" is no longer being used to describe our current Partner Weeks. As a result, the broadcast format closely mimics a regular broadcast, although they have an increased focus on fundraising. 

Why is the 700 Club Broadcasting Looping? 700 Club Interactive is Not Airing.

Over the next couple of weeks, The 700 Club focuses on special breakthrough stories and does not air the 700 Club Interactive. We re-air these special stories so that more people will have the opportunity to see and be changed by them.

Who Gets the Premium, Pledge Express Bonus, Agent-Initiated Increase        

Premium:

The Lord is My Shepherd: A Psalm of David (CD/audio CD)

    Who Gets the Premium:

  • New Partners - Those who become a CBN partner at any giving level.
  • Pledge Express Partners - Partners who give automatically using their credit/debit card or a bank draft from their bank account.
  • Renewing Partners - Mail-in partners who renew their pledge for another year.
  • Partners Upgrading to the Next Giving Level - Partners who increase their giving from one giving level to the next - for example, increasing from $20 (The 700 Club) to $40 (Gold Club).
  • Chairman's Circle and above - our Major Donor partners who give $10,000 or more per year. 

Note: This is a partner exclusive premium.  It is not available to order for non-partners.  If the caller is not a partner and requests a copy of the premium, invite them to join with us and become a partner!

Streaming

All partners can stream the new premium. Partners will either receive an email link to get started or they can sign up for a free account on CBNFamily.com. A CBN.com account is needed to stream or get the MP3 audio download in the CBN Family App or CBNFamily.com.  

Partners can directly access streaming of the new premium by visiting CBN.com/TheLordIsMyShepherd. 

Note: If it is their first time signing in to their CBN.com account, they will also need their CBN partner account number.

There may be times when a partner calls and asks for their CBN account number.

Once the partner has confirmed their identity by verifying their name and address information, you may give them their CBN partner account number. This is the nine-digit number in the Address Information field just above their phone number.
