FAQ's

Am I automatically a CBN Israel Partner if I'm a CBN Monthly Partner?

We actually offer a separate membership program for CBN Israel. When you become a CBN Israel Monthly partner, all funds go directly to the mission of CBN Israel. 

Agent Note: Please share the following statement with the CBN Partners as needed. 

“Approximately 5% of all CBN giving goes toward supporting the work of Israel programs. Given the extensive media evangelism and humanitarian work CBN partners are making possible worldwide, this is a substantial contribution toward our initiatives in Israel.”

How are CBN Israel Funds Distributed?

Approximately 46% of our spending from the CBN Israel fund over the past three years has gone toward funding humanitarian projects aimed at providing relief to Israel’s most vulnerable citizens—including aging Holocaust survivors, victims of terrorism, single moms, lonely refugees, and new immigrants making their prophetic return to Israel. These projects help provide food, basic essentials, and hope in their time of need. 

 

Then, about 38% of our spending from the CBN Israel fund has gone toward producing news and films that reach millions worldwide with the true story of Israel and the Jewish people. These vital media efforts make it possible to help stem the tide of rising global anti-Semitism and hostility toward the Jewish state. This includes the funding of our Jerusalem News Bureau and a portion of Israel's film production costs.

 

Lastly, only 16% of our spending from the CBN Israel fund has gone toward advertising and publicizing the needs of our Israel projects.

What Does My CBN Israel Donation Go Towards?

When you give towards CBN Israel, you are contributing to all of the following:

  1. BLESSING ISRAEL’S PEOPLE IN NEED

You can share God’s love with terror victims, Holocaust survivors, lonely refugees, families in crisis, and more. As the cries for help persist, your support is crucial. You can bring food, shelter, medicine, and other essentials to those struggling in Israel.

  1. REPORTING THE NEWS FROM ISRAEL

Your gift allows us to produce unbiased, independent journalism through broadcasts, podcasts, and online media dedicated to informing the world about what is happening in Israel and the Middle East through our news bureau in Jerusalem.

  1. DOCUMENTING ISRAEL’S TRUE STORY

Your support makes it possible to produce films that tell the true history of Israel, its right to exist in the face of growing anti-Semitism worldwide, its innovative and entrepreneurial culture, and its commitment to helping others—even its enemies.
