Briefing Notes

Release Date: 9/7/23

New Marketing Campaign – Seven Days Ablaze & Live Prayer Event (All Agents) 

From 9/11 - 9/15, viewers can join CBN hosts and staff for CBN’s special Seven Days Ablaze live prayer event. Viewers are invited to join us at 2 PM EST to participate on CBN Family and YouTube.  

To submit prayer requests and participate in live chat, users may visit CBN.com/7DaysAblaze. Users will be required to enter a screen name to participate in live chat (no email address required).  

Release Date: 8/2/23

New Marketing Campaign – CBN’s Seven Days Ablaze (All Agents) 

On 8/4/23, CBN is mailing partners about our upcoming Seven Days Ablaze prayer event, which takes place 9/11 - 9/15. This mailing encourages partners to mail in their prayer requests. The theme for this year's event is "Peace and Power of God’s Heart." 

The mailing includes the following:

  • Brochure entitled, "Peace and Power of God's Heart"
  • Bookmarks (2)
  • Prayer Request Slips 

Partners may call in asking to share their prayer request(s) over the phone or request to order additional brochures, bookmarks, or prayer request slips. This content can be resent by ordering the merch pack.

How will My Prayer Requests be Prayed Over During this Time?

The hosts and staff of CBN will be collectively praying over prayer requests through the following ways:  

  • Mail-in prayer requests received by the requested mail-in date will be distributed around the campus for on-site staff to pray over requests.        
  • Digital prayer requests will be prayed over collectively in inter-departmental prayer groups and live events at noon throughout the week.      
  • Digital Engagement Specialists (DES) will be responding and praying over prayer requests received through the online prayer request form and in the 700 Club YouTube comments section.     
  • CBN staff is praying for all callers & their loved ones. We’re available by phone 24/7 to stand in agreement with you in prayer.

As a New Hire, What Do I Need to Know for Seven Days Ablaze?

Summary:

CBN desires our audience to know that we care about them as individuals. Designating a special week for prayer, known as Seven Days Ablaze, is how we convey this heartfelt message from our ministry. Our entire staff at CBN (across all departments) will be praying for our viewers' needs in a concentrated way.

To accomplish this, we send out mailings to our partners, which includes a brochure and bookmarks as a reminder of our prayer commitment, along with prayer request slips they may return to us.
