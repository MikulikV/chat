FAQ's

Can Donors Who Give to Another Brand Receive the Premium? 

Our Telethon fundraising efforts are centered around joining CBN's general membership where we ask viewers to join at various 700 Club levels. Other brands, such as Operation Blessing, CBN Israel, CBN News, CBN Animation's Superbook, Orphan's Promise, etc., are separate memberships, and do not release the premium, "Divine Direction."

Streaming

All partners can stream the new premium. Partners will either receive an email link to get started or they can sign up for a free account on CBNFamily.com. A CBN.com account is needed to stream or get the MP3 audio download in the CBN Family App or CBNFamily.com.  

Partners can directly access streaming of the new premium by visiting cbn.com/DivineDirectionCD. Along with streaming and MP3 download, partners can access the digital workbook (also known as 21-day daily devotional) inside CBN Family by visiting cbn.com/DailyDirection. 

Note: If it is their first time signing in to their CBN.com account, they will also need their CBN partner account number.

There may be times when a partner calls and asks for their CBN account number.

Once the partner has confirmed their identity by verifying their name and address information, you may give them their CBN partner account number. This is the nine-digit number in the Address Information field just above their phone number.
