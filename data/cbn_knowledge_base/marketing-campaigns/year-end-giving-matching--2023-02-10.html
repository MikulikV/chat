Year-End Giving & Matching

Marketing Article

Written by Tara Jackson | Last published at: February 10, 2023

Description 

With the year coming to a close, the ministry asks viewers to give an end-of-year gift that may be tax-deductible. From December 26th - December 31st, partners will match each donation dollar for dollar.   



Briefing Notes

Update: 2/9/23

Heads Up Announcement – Year-End Giving Statement Option on IVR (All Agents)

The year-end giving statements are being mailed today (2/9/23) via First Class Mail to expedite delivery. Please apologize to the caller for this delay, which was due to technical difficulties.   

Update: 2/6/23

Heads Up Announcement – Year-End Giving Statement Option on IVR (All Agents) 

We have had a delay in mailing the year-end giving statements. Please apologize to the caller for this inconvenience.  

The Operation Blessing year-end giving statements were mailed 2/1/23, and the CBN year-end giving statements are tentatively scheduled to be mailed the week of 2/6/23.  

Also, note that the IVR no longer offers a year-end giving statement option, due to the shift in mailing dates.  

Release Date: 1/4/23

Heads Up Announcement – Year-End Giving Statement Option on IVR (All Agents)   

From 1/4/23 - 4/18/23, the last day to file taxes, the Press 6 option on the IVR will be tailored for those calling about their year-end giving statement. On 800-700-7000, it now says “For year-end giving statements, account, or technical questions, press 6”. For year-end giving statements, callers will hear a message either informing them that statements will be mailed in late January or that the statements have already been mailed. If a caller wants to continue to speak with a representative, they can press 1 and will route to Customer Service.  

For more information on what the message says, click the accordion titled “When Will I Get My Year-End Giving Statement?”.

Release Date: 12/28/22

Process Refresher – 2022 Year End Giving Statements (T2)  

Some callers are starting to request a copy of their 2022 year-end giving statement. As a reminder, statements are mailed at the end of January to donors who have given over $220 and are unable to be sent earlier.  

A new communication will be posted once statements are mailed.  

View the FAQ accordion “When Will I Get My Year End Giving Statement?” for more details and call processing.  

Release Date: 12/21/22

Marketing Campaign – 2023 Year End Giving (T2) 

Effective 12/26-12/31, CBN and Operation Blessing will be matching all donations. In order for a donation to apply towards the matching challenge, it must meet the matching criteria.

Does NOT Qualify for Matching

When Will I Get My Year-End Giving Statement?

Donors who give over $220 per year will be mailed their year-end giving statements in February with the donor's yearly giving amount. If the caller donated to both CBN and Operation Blessing, inform the caller that their statements will be mailed separately.

All other Brands besides OB, such as Orphan's Promise and CBN Israel, will be included on the CBN giving statement.

If a caller wants to know why the total donation amount and the Fair Market Value on their statement are different, please inform the caller that any questions regarding Fair Market Value should be directed to their tax professional. Do not transfer the call to Customer Service. Our Customer Service department is unable to answer any questions regarding Fair Market Value. 

We are unable to provide year-end giving statements prior to late January; however, a caller can request an up-to-date list of their donations as they wait. Please note that an up-to-date list of donations is not an official year-end giving statement and will not include the Fair Market Value. 

If a caller requests an up-to-date receipt or to receive their current total donation amount from a representative, please process these calls as follows:

Category: Account Services 

Project: Request for Receipt 

Agent Note: Select, “Caller Request an Up-to-Date Receipt”. If the caller would like a print or email list of their donations, submit a call referral. To connect with a representative who can review their account and give their donation amount for the year, transfer the call to Customer Service. 

Year-End Giving Statement IVR & Hold Messages:

From 2/17/23 - 4/18/23 (the last day to file taxes), the Press 6 option on the IVR will be tailored for those calling about their year-end giving statement. On 800-700-7000, it now says “For year-end giving statements, account, or technical questions, press 6”. For year-end giving statements, callers will hear a message either informing them that statements will be mailed in late January or that the statements have already been mailed. If a caller wants to continue to speak with a representative, they can press 1 and will route to Customer Service.  

From 2/17/23 - 4/18/23 (the last day to file taxes): 

“Thank you for your generosity! In mid-February, donors who had given over $220 for the year were mailed their year-end giving statement. Statements for CBN and Operation Blessing were mailed separately. For questions regarding the donation amount listed on the statement, we would be happy to review your donation history. For questions regarding the Fair Market Value, please contact a tax professional.  

To continue to speak with a representative, press 1.”
