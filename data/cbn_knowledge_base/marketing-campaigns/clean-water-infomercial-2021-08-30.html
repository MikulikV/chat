Clean Water Infomercial

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: August 30, 2021

Description 

In many places, drinking a sip of water can be extremely hazardous – even deadly. Sadly, many people in developing countries have no other choice but to get their water from contaminated streams or ponds – contamination that causes countless children to die needlessly from diarrhea and typhoid.

Operation Blessing WASH programs (Water, Hygiene, and Sanitation) are helping to change that.

Well Sponsorship

How To Sponsor a Well 

  • Full - one time gift of $4000 (or more)
  • Co - one time gift of $2000
  • Well Project - Monthly gift of $200 for 20 months (This will add up to the cost of one well)
  • If a caller is unable to give the one-time gift, offer the monthly option.
  • If a caller is unable to give on a monthly basis, any donation amount will be greatly appreciated and help to provide water to those who are in need. 
    • Donations will be combined with other gifts and we will build a well in the area where there is the greatest need.

What Do I Receive when I Sponsor a Well?

Information Packet

When you sponsor a well, within 7-14 business days, you will receive a confirmation letter of your donation along with an information packet which includes the following contents:

  • A picture of the water project, matted and suitable for framing
  • An information sheet that tells about the village and the dedication service  (you can visit your well and, in most cases, you can also be present at the well dedication)
  • A small map showing where the village is located   
  • A collage of photographs of the villagers who are served by the clean water 
    • It takes approximately 9-12 months to complete a project. During that time, we hold a dedication ceremony, gather reports and photographs to prepare your packet

Can I Pick the Country for a Well?


All OB wells will be assigned to countries where OB staff has identified as most in need, unless the donor requests a specific country.

Please do not promise that a well can be drilled in a particular country. Please send a call referral to determine if the requested country can be accommodated.


We are unable to drill wells in the U.S. or in countries where we're not currently working.
