FAQ's

What is Superbook Express?

Hosted by everyone's favorite robot, Gizmo, each show features:

  • An exciting Superbook Bible story episode
  • An engaging Bible Background video explaining the story's history and archaeology
  • A Signposts video showing how the story reveals God's plan of salvation
  • A fascinating object lesson demonstrating how God can work in our lives
  • An uplifting music video inspiring kids to sing and praise the Lord
  • A Gospel presentation. 


What's the Difference Between Superbook Express & Superbook Explorer?:

Description:

As a CBN Animation Club member, when will Superbook Express episodes release? 

Members will receive a new release of Superbook Express every month on the CBN Family App and Superbook Kids Bible App. Episodes release on the 1st of the month.

How is SB Express Different from SB Academy Express?

Superbook Express is a streaming-only benefit for CBN Animation Club members only, released on the first of the month. It has no physical DVD component.

Superbook Academy Express has the streaming-only benefit for CBN Animation Club members only (released on the first of the month); but additionally, it includes a physical DVD and Family Resource Guide as well.

When the monthly DVD release will be a SB Academy Express episode, the streaming-only benefit at the first of that month will be the same title.

  • For example, "SB Academy Express: Zacchaeus" will be the streaming-only release on the first of the month. "SB Academy Express: Zacchaeus" will be the same DVD title (and Family Resource Guide title) on the 26th of the month.

When the monthly DVD release will not be a SB Academy Express episode, the streaming-only benefit at the first of that month will be a SB Express title. The DVD on the 26th of that month will be a different title from another series. It could be GizmoGO!, Fruit of the Spirit, Superbook Specials, etc.

  • For example, "Nicodemus" is the SB Express streaming-only title on the first of the month. "GizmoGO! Rig of the West" is the DVD title released on the 26th of the month. The DVD title is not from the SB Academy Express series, so the streaming-only title on the first of the month is SB Express.
