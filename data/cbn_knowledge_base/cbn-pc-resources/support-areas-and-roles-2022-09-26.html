Description 

This article provides information about the various Prayer Center support areas and their roles. 

Technical Support (1-888-778-2000)

What They Do

  • The WFM Technical Support Team is responsible for day-to-day technical issues within the Prayer Center. They monitor individual technical issues, as well as major incidents affecting many agents. They also connect with CBN's IT department on a case-by-case basis on behalf of Prayer Center staff.
  • For general technical issues, agents and coaches can post a comment under that day's thread in the Technical Support Teams Channel.

What They Do

WFM handles all analytical aspects of the contact center and manages the key performance indicators (KPIs). Including Intraday Support, WFM is a team of analysts that handle scheduling, planning and intraday management. WFM also handles equipment and timekeeping.

Quality 

What They Do

Quality is responsible for measuring the outcome of our performance during calls and partner/user interactions in order to ensure the quality of our performance is meeting and exceeding the standards and expectations we have set as a ministry. By doing this, they help our ministry stay focused on always excelling in our mission of sharing the love of Jesus through compassionate prayer ministry and outstanding service.

CEE Team 

What They Do

CEE stands for Communication, Engagement, and Experience. The CEE Team works to ensure that all staff of the Prayer Center are properly equipped with the knowledge and tools necessary to thrive as an employee. This includes monitoring of the following areas:

  • Internal Communications and Announcements
  • The PC Hub
  • The Weekly Dose of Vitamin CEE Videos on CEE TV
  • Coordinating Virtual Events, Parties, Celebrations, Activities, and More!

The CEE Team also works to cultivate an environment that is fun, well-connected, and inviting. To do this, they partner with all other departments of the Prayer Center to ensure communication is clear across all avenues.
