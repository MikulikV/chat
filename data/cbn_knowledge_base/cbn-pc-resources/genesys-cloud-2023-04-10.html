FAQs - Troubleshooting

What If I Am Not Getting a Screen Pop or Any Calls?

  • Please do NOT use the workaround of hitting a different status in Scripter when it's frozen on the wait screen during a live interaction.
  • Use the following steps to ensure any issues with screen pops are statused correctly for tracking.
    • Engage with the caller using your awesome GENESIS call skills without your Scripter screen.
    • While engaging, if you need to put someone on hold, mute yourself, or hang up the call, use the "talking man" icon in Genesys Cloud.
    • Once the call ends, DO NOT SELECT THE WRAP-UP CODE IN GENESYS CLOUD. Instead, go back to Scripter Cloud and select, "Logout of the System."
    • Log back into Scripter Cloud. A message should pop up alerting you to close/complete your previous interaction. Click "ok" and the correct wrap up code will automatically status in Genesys Cloud.

  • If for some reason you do the steps above and still do not get another call, please partner with your coach/tech support.Â 
  • For any other issues that may occur, partner with coach/tech support as normal.

How Can I Change My Hours of Availability?

New hires cannot request changes to their availability until they have been in their position for 6 months. From there, if you request an availability change, these are viewed as requests and are subject to approval.

If it is approved, and you determine you need to make another change again in the future, you must wait an additional 6 months to make another change to your availability.
