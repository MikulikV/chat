Management Levels (Chain of Command)

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: May 23, 2022

Description 

This article provides information about the Prayer Center management levels and their associated roles. 

Management Levels (Chain of Command)

Assistant Coach

Assistant Coaches assist in helping agents for day to day tasks, processes and quality.

Reports to Lead Coach. 

Coach

Coaches are directly responsible for a team of agents delivering outstanding customer service and compassionate prayer ministry one interaction at a time. 

Coaches are also responsible for agents accountability on assignments or tasks. Coaches are the first point of contact for any agent concerns. 

Reports to Coaching Manager.

Delete

Lead Coach

A Lead Coach is directly responsible to help Coaches move their team strategy forward, in unity with the overall department strategy. A Lead Coach provides coaching to Coaches. 

Reports to Coaching Manager. 

Delete

Coaching Managers

Coaches are responsible for the development of coaches for the empowerment and development of agents. Responsible for systems and processes that enable us to deliver outstanding customer service and compassionate prayer ministry. 

Coaching Managers work in unity with other managers on the leadership team (Training, WFM, Customer Experience). Coaching Managers are the second point of contact for any agent concerns that were unable to resolved with the Coach.

Reports to Strategic Director.

Delete

Strategic Director

The Strategic Director is responsible for the overall direction, budgeting, and operation plan approvals of the Prayer Center.

Reports to Vice President. 

Delete

VP
(Vice President)

The Vice President is responsible for executive level vision, direction, and approvals across multiple departments. 

Reports to CEO/Gordon Robertson.
