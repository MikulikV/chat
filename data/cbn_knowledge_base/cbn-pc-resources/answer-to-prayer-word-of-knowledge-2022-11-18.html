Answer to Prayer - Word of Knowledge

Written by Tara Jackson | Last published at: November 18, 2022

Description 

Every week, the Prayer Center receives dozens of calls from individuals who share their amazing testimonies! We love to encourage viewers with these testimonies by sharing them throughout our ministry, whether on-air, through print, digital media, or video presentation. 


Briefing Notes

Release Date: 11/21/22

Internal Process Change – Answer to Prayer Digital Consent Form (All Agents)  

Beginning 11/23/22, the Answer to Prayer/ Word of Knowledge and C7H Answer to Prayer/ Word of Knowledge projects will be updated to promote the digital consent form. This ensures the ministry can receive consent to share testimonies in a timely manner. The print form will only be sent to callers if they do not want the digital form when asked or do not have an email address.   


Release Date: 5/18/22

Internal Process Change - New Answer to Prayer/Word of Knowledge Scripts (All Agents)

Starting 5/23/22, the Answer to Prayer/Word of Knowledge and C7H Answer to Prayer/Word of Knowledge projects will updated to the newest version of the script. At this time, agents will no longer be required to gather a caller's full testimony, but will rather document a few key details and send the caller a consent form for CBN to use the testimony within various media outlets (print, digital, and/or broadcast).


Highlights

  • Other Info: 
  • Our viewers love hearing what God has done in the lives of others. As a result, the Prayer Center partners alongside our CBN Producers to ask for permission to share these wonderful testimonies so that they can encourage others through on-air, print, digital media, or film presentations. 
  • Prior to releasing a caller's testimony to CBN Producers, the ministry must first receive a signed consent form. This ensures that both the ministry & the caller has a record of the information shared.
  • Callers have the option to receive the consent form via email or print. 
    • Email Option: This is the fastest & easiest option. The caller will receive an email immediately with a link to an internet form (website). On the website, the user will use the checkbox and textbox fields to give their consent (no e-signature required). Once the user gives their consent, both the ministry and the user will receive an email notification of the submission.
    • Print Option: The caller will receive a print copy where they will write their testimony and check off consent. Included in the merch pack is a return envelope. 
  • (Disclaimer) Due to the high volume of testimonies we receive, there is no guarantee CBN producers can use every one.
  • Product Codes:
    • English: 
      • ATPF (Print - Merch Pack) - Consent Form, Cover Letter
      • EATP (Email/Internet Form)
    • Spanish:
      • 7HAP (Print - Merch Pack) - Consent Form, Cover Letter
      • E7HP (Email/Internet Form)

I Submitted the Consent Form, Have You Received It?

PSR 1 & PSR 2s:
"Thank you for sharing your testimony with us! I would be happy to transfer you to our Customer Service Department. They will be able to research whether we have received the returned consent form."

Category: Transfers
Project: CBN Transfers*

Agent Note: Select Customer Service Transfers and provide adequate referral notes detailing the caller's request to know the status of the consent form.

Example: "Mrs. Chapman submitted her ATP consent form on 00/00/00. She wants to know whether the ministry has received the returned form."

PCS:
"Thank you for sharing your testimony with us! I would be happy to look into your account for you."

Below are the following steps for seeing whether the consent form was received:
1. Go into CRM and look up the caller's account. 
2. Once in the account, select the tab that says Pledge - Pages - Activities - Cases.
3. Review the Activities/Notes section and look for a header that starts with Letter From... or Email From... and gauge the date based on when the caller shared they returned the form. 

Agent Note: Letter from means the caller mailed back the print consent form. Email from means the caller used the internet version.

4. If you are unable to find the caller's consent email or letter in CRM, email ATPConsent@cbn.org. 

"Thank you for allowing me the pleasure of assisting you today. I’ve sent an inquiry to the appropriate team and you should receive a response within the next 24-48 business hours." (Weekend and holidays permitting)

What Information Will Be Shared When I Give My Consent? 

"CBN values your privacy. We want to assure you that no detailed personal information will be shared other than first name, city, and possibly age."

Will I Be Notified If My Testimony Is Selected? 

CBN producers will contact those who are selected to film a video presentation of their testimony. However, testimonies to be used on air, print, or digital media are not notified.
