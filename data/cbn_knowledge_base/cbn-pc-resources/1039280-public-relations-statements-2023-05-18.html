The statement and email are located in the "Political" accordion below.

PR statements must be shared when callers express concern or would like to engage in general, political, medical, or financial conversations.

Medical 

Statement: "While the 700 Club hosts may offer commentary on news coverage, CBN neither supports nor opposes taking a particular medical treatment and is unable to provide medical advice." 

While the Christian Broadcasting Network serves to report medical news coverage from various perspectives, the Christian Broadcasting Network is unable to encourage callers to take medical treatments or offer medical advice. CBN encourages viewers to contact their health care professionals when making a medical decision.

COVID-19 Examples 

Below are a few statement options to assist you in the occurrence that you receive a call: 

Note: Agents can use discretion on general medical calls.  

  • I understand that there are different opinions about the COVID-19 vaccines as well as their testing, safety, efficacy, and ethics. While CBN neither supports nor opposes taking the COVID-19 vaccines and is unable to provide medical advice, we encourage you to speak with your health care professional when making any medical decision.      
  • Thank you for sharing your concerns. I can understand how overwhelming this pandemic has been for all of us and the importance of making the decision of whether to take the vaccine. Although, CBN neither supports nor opposes taking the COVID-19 vaccines and is unable to provide medical advice I will be happy to agree with you for the Lord’s perfect will to be accomplished.”     
  • I understand how this is important to you and I will be happy to pray with you. While CBN neither supports nor opposes taking the COVID-19 vaccines and is unable to provide medical advice, we absolutely can pray for God’s guidance on this important decision and that your health care professional provides medical advice according to God’s leading. Let’s pray...     
  • I understand how the possibility of a vaccine can be frustrating and even cause us to be angry. CBN neither supports nor opposes taking the COVID-19 vaccines and is unable to provide medical advice but, I would be honored to pray the Lord’s will be done and for wisdom for those in leadership as they make key decisions for our nation.
