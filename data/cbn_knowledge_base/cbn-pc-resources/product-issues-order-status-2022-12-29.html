Product Issues - Order Status

Agent Resources

Written by Tara Jackson | Last published at: December 29, 2022

Description 

The Product Issues project allows agents to provide outstanding customer service by allowing agents to view the status of a caller's order and escalate the issue to the appropriate department. 

Through the use of the Product Order History table, all agents have the ability to share the order status of caller's product whether ordered by phone, mail, or online.

New Internal Process Change - Product Issues & Order Statuses (All Agents)

On 4/29/22, the Prayer Center will be launching a new project titled Product Issues! This project should be used to report any product issues such as: item damaged, never received, missing an item, received the wrong item, etc. 

All agents will have the ability to review a caller's order status and escalate the issue to the appropriate team if necessary (Customer Service or Superbook Partner Assistance). 

At this time, the Product/Premium/MBR Kits Order Problems and Superbook Product Issues projects will no longer be visible in Scripter. 

Highlights

  • The Product Issues project is used to process a caller's request when they have an issue with their product such as:
    • The caller requests an update on the status of their order
    • The caller has not received their product
    • The caller received the wrong product
    • The product arrived damaged and the caller is requesting a replacement
    • Items are missing from the product kit
  • You will need to check the caller's product order history before processing the caller's product issue request.

FAQ's

How Do I Check The Order Status?

Please process these calls as follows:

Category: Account Services
Project: Product Issues *

  1. Verify the caller's account. 
  2. View the Product Order History table to assist with order status inquiry. 
    • If the caller is satisfied with the update, select Inquiry Only. 
    • If the caller's order requires further support, proceed by selecting the appropriate script option (General Products, Superbook Products, Superbook Academy) and submit the attached referral. 
      • In the referral notes include the Product Name, Product Issue (Damaged Item, Never Received, Missing an Item, etc.), and Order Date (if applicable). 

What To Do If the Caller Appears As 999/Product History Table Is Blank?

There are times when a person may call the ministry using a different number than the one associated with their account. As as a result, they will appear as 999 in our system. To pull up the caller's account, use the Account Lookup tool located on the left navigation bar. 
Once the account is pulled up, the caller's order details will appear in the Product Order History table.

Note: If the Product Order History table still appears as blank, the order may still be processing. It takes 24 hours for a new order to go from CRM and display in the table. 

What Products Will Display in the Product Order History Table?

Any product associated with a person's account will appear in the Product Order History table. This includes products ordered through the phones (ordered manually or automatically ordered on the backend), online, by mail, or membership premiums that are sent to our partners as a thank-you gift. 

Agents can search past orders from within the past 3 years.
