FAQ's

Who is a Major Donor?

A major donor is someone who gives 10K or more per year (one-time gift or cumulative giving throughout the year).

Agent Note

In Scripter, you can identify a Major Donor by looking at their name and address information at the top of the screen. A Major Donor's information will be in a red box in stead of green.


However, if a Major Donor is yet to give during the current year, it may be possible that their information is still being shown in a green box. If they mention anything implying that they could have been a Major Donor in the past or present, it is best to treat them as if they are.
