What is a PCS Agent (Partner Care Specialist)

Partner Care is a specialized team in the Prayer Center whose agents are called Partner Care Specialists.

A Partner Care Specialist's work consists of primarily nurturing relationships with our callers through finding solutions to their issues or concerns.

Special inbound customer service lines route to the Partner Care team as well as customer service transfers.

Hours of Operation: Partner Care is available from 9 AM to 9 PM EST, Monday through Friday. Partner Care Specialists whose schedules start before 9 AM or end after 9 PM will engage in regular Prayer Center work outside of Partner Care hours of operation.Â
