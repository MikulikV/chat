Lockdown Procedures

Step 1: Immediate STOP Working & Proceed to a Safe Room 

CBN will notify all employees by text and email with the following message: 

“This is a Lockdown Emergency Warning.  Go to the nearest room and lock the door.  I repeat, go to the nearest room and lock the door.” 

  • Find a safe room - any room without windows and a door that can be locked 

Step 2: Secure the Safe Room

  • Close and lock the door
  • Turn out the lights
  • Barricade the door

Step 3: Control All Movement & Sound

  • Turn off cellphone
  • Refrain from talking or working on your computer
  • Hide under a desk or workstation (if possible)

Step 4: Wait for the “All Clear” Announcement

Note: The “All Clear” announcement means that the threat/intruder has left the building

The Introductory Period for new employees is the first 90-days of employment.  Agents are expected to maintain good attendance and punctuality. 

If you are unable to work due to illness or an unforeseen circumstance, you must contact the Intraday Support Desk before the beginning of your shift each day that you are absent. The Intraday Support Desk phone number is: 

1-84-INTRADAY 
(1-844-687-2329) 

After reporting the reason for your absence to the Intraday Support Desk, you will then be transferred to your Coach.  CBN reserves the right to require a doctor’s note for any absence due to illness.

Weekend Rotation

Agents who do not work permanent weekends will work a weekend rotation.  The weekend rotation schedule is found in Verint.  Agents are given a day off during the week before the assigned weekend to work. 

For example: You are assigned to work Saturday, March 22nd and have been given Thursday, March 20th as your day off.

Three-Phase Program

Full-time Agents who currently work both weekend days every week, but are interested in working fewer weekend days can participate in the Three-Phase Program: 

  • Phase Zero: 2 weekend days per week 
  • Phase One: 1 weekend day per week  
  • Phase Two: 1 weekend day every two weeks  
  • Phase Three: 1 weekend day every four weeks

Here’s how it works: 

  1. Once an Agent has been working in a given Phase for six months with excellent performance statistics for at least 3 consecutive months, Agents can request a Weekend Phase Change Request in Verint. 
    • Agents must have availability on Saturdays and Sundays.
  2. New hires are automatically entered into Phase Zero when they are hired and can apply for Phase One after six months. 
  3. Leadership will review three months of performance for each Agent who submits a Weekend Phase Change Request to determine eligibility.
  4. Agents will receive a notification on leadership's decision within 5 business days from submission.  
  5. If an Agent is approved to go to the next Phase, the Agent’s schedule will be changed between then and the next published schedule.

Holiday Scheduling 

All Agents can expect to work some holidays during the year.  Holidays are defined as either “Actual” or “Declared”.  

  • “Actual” holidays are calendar holidays and include the 4th of July, Thanksgiving and Christmas.  
  • “Declared” holidays are days that CBN announces as holidays and include Good Friday, the day after Thanksgiving and Christmas Eve.

Learning Breaks

Whenever a learning break is on your schedule, you are expected to use that time engaged in the assigned learning break activity. Using learning breaks as break time is unacceptable. 


The daily 10-minute learning break on every Agent’s schedule is time given to you to prepare for taking phone calls. 


Required
Optional Activities
  • Read and respond to emails
  • Practice new projects on the Training Account
  • Read Briefing Notes
  • Retake training modules that have already been taken
  • Check your schedule in Verint for meal breaks, Chapel and Learning Breaks
  • Read and respond to Yammer posts


Using the 10-minute learning break for any other purpose such as going to the bathroom, getting refreshments, or arriving to work late is unacceptable. 


If you finish the learning break activities early, resume your phone work unless otherwise scheduled. 

The 5-minute learning break at the end of your shift is exclusively for completing your Chronicle timesheet.
