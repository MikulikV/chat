What is Gift Aid?

If you’re a taxpayer in the UK, Gift Aid allows CBN Europe to reclaim tax on donations you’ve made to us. That means for every £1 you have donated; the government will give us an extra 25p – at no further cost to you.

For more additional information on gift aid, click here. 

How Can I Participate in the Eternal Wall of Answered Prayers? 

Eternal Wall of Answered Prayer is a huge Christian landmark. It will sit in the heart of the UK by the end of 2023. The gigantic monument of hope will host a million accounts of answered prayer, making it the largest database of hope stories in the world.

CBN Europe is partnering with Eternal Wall of Answered Prayer through financial support and taking calls on behalf of the Eternal Wall to collect answered prayers and donations. 

Answers to Prayer can be submitted online at cbneurope.com/eternal-wall/ or by calling the Answer to Prayer line (0300 561 0080 or UK or +44300 561 0080 for outside the UK. The lines will be open Monday to Friday between 9am to 5pm. 

What is CBN Europe doing in Ukraine?

We have teams on the ground right now in the Ukraine, providing emergency food, water, blankets, electricity generators. We are continuing to support our vulnerable children projects in spite of the dangers. Aid is desperately needed as this is fast becoming Europe’s largest refugee crisis in modern times with hundreds of thousands of people already displaced from their homes. 

For more information visit cbneurope.com/ukraine/
