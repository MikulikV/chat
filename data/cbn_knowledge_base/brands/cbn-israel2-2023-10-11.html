Key Areas of Work

Holocaust Survivors

CBN Israel joins the Jewish community and other caring people around the world in remembering the victims of the Holocaust and honoring the Jewish men and women who miraculously survived Hitler’s genocide.

Nearly 200,000 Holocaust survivors live in Israel today, and tens of thousands of them live in poverty and isolation. Those who live in poor conditions can hardly afford food, medicine, and other essentials. Often, these aging survivors do not have family or loved ones to care for them on a regular basis.

Supporting CBN Israel provides these aging survivors with food, medicine, walkers, and regular visits that remind them they are not forgotten, and they are not alone.

Victims of Terrorism

While terrorists are rewarded for killing or injuring innocent Israelis, CBN Israel rushes relief to the victims.  

The people who live along Israel’s dangerous borders have suffered the effects of unrelenting bombings, illegal invasion tunnels, fire kite/balloon attacks, and continual emergency situations. The ongoing threat of terrorism causes these families and communities to live in a constant state of anxiety and high alert. They have 15 seconds to get to a secure location every time a siren goes off, all hours of the day and night.

Supporting CBN Israel provides immediate aid to families directly affected by terror attacks, bomb shelters for communities in high-risk areas, and trauma counseling to victims who are suffering from this terrible violence.

Single Mothers

There are many single mothers living in Israel, who are struggling to make it. Many of them have been abandoned or have come out of abusive relationships. 

Supporting CBN Israel provides them with groceries, housing, medical care, financial aid, and job training. It also gives these moms hope and encouragement.

Immigrant Families

There are many Jewish families who are seeking refuge in Holy Land, and others who are simply wanting to return to the land of their ancestors. Yet, many of them often have no one to turn to for assistance.

These new immigrant families face the monumental challenges of learning the Hebrew language, acclimating to Israeli society, and finding stable jobs.

Supporting CBN Israel provides families with groceries, housing, medical care, financial aid, and job training, as well as comfort and friendship.

Orphans and Children

There are thousands of Israeli orphans and children who live in poverty.

Because of Israel’s increased defense spending and ongoing need to protect its people from war and terrorism, many of these critical needs often go unmet.   

Supporting CBN Israel provides needy orphans and children with food, clothing, housing, and medical care, as well as love and hope.

Delete

Lone Soldiers

There are many lone soldiers who have no family in Israel or place to call home. Most soldiers on leave have the advantage of going home to their families, getting a homecooked meal, and staying in their own rooms.

Not having a family to go home to and a place that is yours can leave you feeling even more isolated and alone during your military service. 

Supporting CBN Israel provides a home base for these soldiers, a place of refuge from the stresses and demands of life in the military. The home is fully equipped with all necessary furniture and appliances.

Financial Counseling

There are thousands of new immigrants, single parents, young couples, and large families who struggle each month to make ends meet.

CBN Israel has an extraordinary team with various degrees in business, finance, and even family counseling. They are truly some of the best financial advisors and social workers you can find in Israel.

Supporting CBN Israel makes it possible to give financial counseling to hundreds of parents and families.

Business Development

The lifeblood of a flourishing community is thriving businesses that meet real needs, solve real problems, and provide stable jobs for hard-working families.

This is especially true in Israel, but often the conditions can prove even more challenging and competitive than in other countries.

CBN Israel provides seminars, training, and counseling to help aspiring entrepreneurs as well as struggling business owners in Israel.
