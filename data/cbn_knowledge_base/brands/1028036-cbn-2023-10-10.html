Brand Article

Written by Jessica Mowry | Last published at: October 10, 2023

Description 

CBN is a multifaceted nonprofit organization that provides programming by cable, broadcast and satellite to over 100 countries worldwide, with a 24-hour telephone prayer line. Chief among CBN's broadcasting is The 700 Club, a daily television program featuring Gordon Robertson, Terry Meeuwsen, Ashley Key, Andrew Knox, Wendy Griffith, and news anchor John Jessup. 




Briefing Notes

Release Date: 10/11/23

New Marketing Campaign – CBN Fence & Unity Mailing (All Agents) 

On 10/16/23, CBN will be mailing a special letter to promote our new Fence & Unity campaign. The recipients will be donors who are “fenced” to certain brands, yet have never given to CBN specifically. Therefore, the purpose of this mailing is to bridge the gaps that exist between the different brands. 

For example, a Superbook donor may not know about CBN and what the ministry does as a whole. Since CBN facilitates what flows to the other brands, we want donors to know that we are united as one ministry.  

The mailing will be marked as an “urgent, emergency letter from Gordon,” which has a Thanksgiving holiday theme, asking for one-time gifts to be returned by 11/22/23 to rush help to those in need.

Pledge Express

Pledge Express is a secure, electronic way for donors to give monthly. Pledge Express can be set up using a credit card, debit card, or electronic funds transfer (EFT). When a donor becomes a monthly partner online at cbn.com, they are automatically enrolled in Pledge Express. 


Pledge Express Partners Receive: 

  • The most recent premium (CD/DVD/Book) that's released every Partner Week.
  • A monthly teaching CD from the series titled "Power for Life" which covers topics like marriage and family, finances, Bible prophecy, and much more--mailed around the 24th or 25th of each month. Power for Life teachings are also available to stream online at CBN.com/PowerforLife. 
  • Access to the CBN Partner Video Library (streaming) with current CBN TV episodes, faith-based movies, Bible-based teachings and kids' shows, as well as powerful teachings from Pat and Gordon Robertson.
  • A semi-annual magazine titled "Miracle Living Today."
  • New: Pledge Express members will also receive the most recent pledge express bonus CD/DVD.

Can I Copy Material from CBN?

Generally, CBN does not allow the copying of its materials that are mailed or available online, other than to download and print an article or fact sheet for personal use. 

If someone wants to make copies of certain material for a study group or wider distribution at a church, or to copy an online video for exhibition to others, s/he would have to send a letter or email to CBN explaining the circumstances of how the material is to be used and what quantities are needed. 

Copying of DVDs would require written permission for any purpose. 

In summary, unless a single copy of written material or an online video is to be made for strictly personal use, written permission must be obtained from CBN.

When Did Pat Robertson Retire? 

On The 700 Club’s 60th anniversary (10/1/2021), Pat Robertson, founder of the Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN), announced his retirement from hosting The 700 Club.

Pat Robertson will continue to appear on our monthly interactive episodes of The 700 Club called Your Questions Honest Answers. He will also remain available as a senior consultant on international affairs, appearing occasionally as news warrants.

Robertson also looks forward to developing his energy and experience full-time to helping train and equip members of the 11,000 strong student body of Regent University as they are preparing their mission to become “Christian Leaders to Change the World”.

What is CBN's Mission Statement?

The mission of CBN and its affiliated organizations is to prepare the nations of the world for the coming of Jesus Christ and the establishment of the Kingdom of God on earth. Our ultimate goal is to achieve a time in history when “The knowledge of the Lord will cover the earth as the waters cover the sea.”
