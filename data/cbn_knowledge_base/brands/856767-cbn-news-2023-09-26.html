What Makes CBN News Unique?

First, we are a Christian news network. CBN News has been presenting news through the lens of faith for decades. When you watch our programs, read our articles, or listen to our podcasts, you won't be bombarded with skewed statistics or a reporter's personal viewpoints. Our journalists focus on presenting fact-based truth - all through the lens of faith.

Secondly, because of those distinctives, CBN News often reports on stories that you aren't likely to read or hear about anywhere else. You get important information about what is happening in the U.S. and around the world - breaking news, politics, health, and entertainment news. We have new bureaus in Virginia Beach, VA, Washington, D.C., and in Jerusalem to cover the Middle East.

Additionally, as a non-profit news organization that is part of the Christian Broadcasting Network, you get all our content free, 24-hours-a-day, 7 days a week.

How can I watch?

CBN News is offered for free 24/7.

The schedule can be viewed on WatchCBNNews.com. This is our Finder page where viewers may search by zip code. On the left column, you'll see results for every place in your local area that CBN News can be found on satellite, cable, or over-the-air.

If not available in your area yet, you'll see other places you can watch CBN News, including online, streaming platforms, and apps.

Roku, Apple TV, and Amazon Fire TV require you to add the CBN News App onto your main menu on that streaming platform. Options include:

  • Download the CBN News App on your television on the main streaming menu
  • Go to CBNNewsApp.com - then click on your streaming service for more information and to download the app.

YouTube TV:

  • A search will take you to the CBN News channel on YouTube.

On iPhone or Android phones:

  • Download the CBN News App from the Apple Store or from the Google Play Store.

The link to watch CBN News on the app from an iPhone, Android, tablet, or streaming television: CBN.com/News/app
