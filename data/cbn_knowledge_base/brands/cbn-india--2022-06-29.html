CBN India

Description 

CBN India is an organization that builds communities of hope mainly through media by documenting real life stories and messages of hope in the face of adversity. 

CBN India media content provides a moral compass for those lost in the darkness of despair. It is headquartered in Gurugram (Delhi-NCR) with a network of stringers across the country.

What resources does CBN India offer? 

  • Superbook Bible Resources for Children - Superbook is the Bible brought to life through an animated TV series—a timeless form of storytelling—to introduce children to Jesus and to help develop their faith.

Apart from being a TV series based on Biblical truths, Superbook lets children experience and interact with Bible stories using the Superbook Bible App and the Superbook Kids website. Both are engaging and powerful tools accessible in any place, at any time. 
Superbook is so much more than entertainment for children. Where these animated episodes end, life-changing stories begin. 

For more information or to download content, visit cbnindia.org/superbook

  • Devotional Resources - Resources on how to pray, music for your soul, ways to join in on live prayer sessions and engaging with the Bible. 


  • Self Help Resources - Real life stories to encourage and includes topics such as hope, dealing with failure, healing in relationships, finances, mental health, addiction and more.
