FAQ's

What ministries does CBN Asia have? 

  • The 700 Asia - The program airs every weekdays, 12 midnight, on GMA 7. It has also extended its ministry through CBN Asia Media Center, and its social media accounts on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and YouTube.  
  • Superbook - Partners with churches, local governments, schools, and private organizations in a caravan tour to bring the love of Jesus to more children in different communities here and abroad. It also developed a vacation Bible kit called KampEON which aims to share God’s love to children and teach them using His Word through vacation Bible outreaches. 
  • The Flying House - A children’s anime series produced by the Christian Broadcasting Network and dubbed in Tagalog. Through a house that flies and works as a time machine, The Flying House carries an absent-minded professor, three fun-loving kids, and a quirky little robot to action-packed adventures in biblical times. 
  • Oyayi - CBN Asia’s children’s TV program produced in partnership with the National Council for Children’s Television. 
  • Reverb Worship PH -  Celebrates and declares the unchanging love of God during changing times and give Christian artists a platform to create and perform songs that worship God and inspire people. 
  • Asian Center for Missions - A Missionary Training Program that equips and sends missionaries to least reached people. 
  • Operation Blessing Philippines - Established in 1966 to reach out to the most neglected and disadvantaged people in the Philippines. 
  • CBN Asia Prayer Center- The CBN Asia Prayer Center is available 24/7, seven days a week and has satellite centers in Cebu and General Santos cities for our Visayan-speaking kababayans. It also provides hotlines for the US and Canada.

(For SMS Only) - Only text messages can be received from this number.
