Ten Ways You Can Pray For Israel Prayer Guide

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: October 25, 2021

Description 

Ten Ways You Can Pray for Israel, written by Arlene Bridges Samuels, guides readers to pray for Israel, either in their own way, or with 10 biblical figures who have been curated for this prayer guide. In chronological order, the biblical figures are: 
  • Moses
  • Deborah
  • Ruth
  • Nehemiah
  • Esther
  • Isaiah
  • Daniel
  • Zephaniah
  • The Apostle Paul
  • Cornelius
