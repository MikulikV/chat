Product Announcement - Written in Stone: Kings & Prophets (T2)

On 3/7/22, CBN will mail partners about the upcoming release of the 4th DVD in the Written in Stone series, Kings and Prophets. For a donation of any dollar amount, donors will receive the physical DVD along with instant access to 4k streaming of the film. The release will be promoted on-air & online starting 3/14/22.
