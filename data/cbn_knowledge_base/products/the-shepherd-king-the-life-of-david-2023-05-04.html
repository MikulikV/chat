The Shepherd King: The Life of David

Product Article

Written by Glena Fitzmorris | Last published at: May 04, 2023

Description 

In this 4-part CD/audiobook, "The Shepherd King: The Life of David" and accompanying devotional, "After God's Own Heart: A Ten Day Devotional from the Psalms of David," Pat Robertson shares rich insights into the incredible story of King David. 

As you listen to this epic of Biblical history, discover profound spiritual insights about the life of one of the Bible's most beloved heroes and prophetic voices.
