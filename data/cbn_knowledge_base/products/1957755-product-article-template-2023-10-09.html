What Does "Oracle" Mean?

By dictionary definition, oracle refers to "a person or thing regarded as an infallible authority or guide on something."

These are the following quotes used in promoting "Oracles of God: The Story of the Old Testament" on CBN's broadcasts:

  • "There were those who collected the oral and written traditions."
  • "It mattered to them to get it right."
  • "If you can't rely on its history, how can you rely on its theology?"

The promotion emphasizes the extreme effort that historians and theologians put forth in verifying the accuracy of historical documents, from which we now have the Old Testament.
