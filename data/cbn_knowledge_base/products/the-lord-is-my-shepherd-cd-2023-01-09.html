The Lord is My Shepherd CD

Product Article

Description 

In The Lord is My Shepherd: A Psalm of David CD, Gordon Robertson takes the listener on a journey through the beloved and memorized portions of the Bible. This verse-by-verse analysis unlocks the Hebrew meaning behind the words and will bring you comfort and encouragement in your walk with the Lord.
