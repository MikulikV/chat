C7H - El Libro De La Esperanza/The Book of Hope

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: October 19, 2022

Description 

El Libro De La Esperanza/The Book of Hope helps readers discover joy, peace, the power of prayer, and the promise of eternal life. Many people rely on their possessions, pleasures, and accomplishments to find satisfaction and fulfillment in this life. The Book of Hope shows how the happiness that these come from these things is only temporary.  
 

The Book of Hope will help to discover true joy that will last a lifetime. Readers will be filled with an exciting new sense of purpose and direction in their lives.
