Release Date: 5/23/23

New Product Announcement – “Oracles of God: The Story of the Old Testament” DVD (T2) 

On 5/26/23, CBN will be promoting the new release from CBN Films, “Oracles of God: The Story of the Old Testament” DVD. It will be available on 6/5/23. This is a fascinating documentary that explores how the Holy Bible was written from the beginning of recorded history to Moses and the Old Testament prophets.  

Callers may request a single DVD for a donation of any amount, or a 5-pack for a donation of $25 or more. Be sure to capture the caller’s email address. When the physical copy of the DVD(s) is ordered, Scripter will automatically send the caller a link to access streaming.
