Helping the Home Front

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: October 09, 2023

Description 

Helping the Home Front provides financial assistance to churches so it can better support and disciple military families through multiple and lengthy deployments.

After identifying unexpected hardships within active duty military families, Helping the Home Front partners with local churches to meet those needs.

FAQs

How does CBN's Helping the Home Front Work?

The Christian Broadcasting Network gives donations to churches who identify military families in their congregation needed financial help.

What Are Some of the Ways You Have Helped?

  • Emergency travel expenses
  • Assistance with medical bills and equipment
  • Mortgages
  • Utilities
  • Home repairs
  • Heating and air conditioning units
  • Appliances
  • Furniture
  • Vehicle repairs
  • Back-to-school items
  • Moving expenses
  • Assistance with adoption fees
Helping the Homefront's vision also includes partnering with ministries who are helping service members deal with Post Traumatic Stress.
Delete

What is the Application Process?

Helping the Home Front works in partnership with local churches. A brief email from your church explaining the family's need is all that's necessary to begin the approval process. The Contact Us page is located here and must be filled out by a pastor or ministry leader. 

HHF gives donations to churches and ministries only.

(Note: Financial assistance cannot be given directly to individuals)

Is Helping the Home Front Only Open to Active Duty?

This program is designed to honor active duty and their families. 

*Exceptions for veterans will be made on a case-by-case basis. 

Are Single Active Duty Military Members Eligible?

If a single active duty member is responsible for someone in their household (child/parent/sibling, etc.) they will be considered.

How Long Will it Take Before CBN Helps My family?

Once approved, the process is generally completed within a few weeks.

How Does my Church Receive Money?

CBN will send a check to your church for the approved funds. Your church will be responsible for providing CBN a simple accounting of how the funds were distributed.
