Process Refresher - Escalating Superbook Academy Issues (PCS)

As we support Superbook Academy and receive calls that require escalation to the Superbook Academy department, it is important to gather information about that particular Academy account administrator. 
 
Our Superbook Academy department can only locate an Academy account with the name of the church, or the Academy administrator's email address. The administrator of the Academy subscription is the person who created the account, set up the billing and would have sent out the teacher links. It cannot be the email address of one of the teachers who may be calling with the issue. 
 
Also, keep in mind that we may receive calls where the person simply says they have a Superbook issue - a best practice will be to clarify if they are members of the CBN Animation club or part of Academy, to avoid confusion and submitting incorrect referrals. 
 

Please process these calls as follows: 
 
Category: Product 
Project: Superbook Academy 
Referral: Superbook Academy Accounts 

In the following details in the referral please note: 

  • Church Name 
  • Administrators email address 
  • Description of issue or error message that they may have received

Course Content

Your subscription to Superbook Academy includes instant access to a growing number of courses, with each lesson featuring Leader Guides for Large Group, Small Group, and Additional Activities, as well as the following exclusive videos: 

  • The Bible Background video, showing the archaeological, historical, and cultural context of each course
  • The Signposts video, revealing how each Bible story points to Christ and how He fulfills biblical prophecy
  • The Condensed Bible Story video, which can be shown in Lessons 2 and 3 for children who missed the full Bible story video in Lesson 1
  • The Salvation Poem music video, presenting God’s plan through Jesus in a way children can easily understand
  • Original music videos, and more.

What is the difference between Superbook Academy and Superbook Club?

Each Superbook Academy course is based on one Superbook episode and consists of three lessons. Every course includes three full lessons – each lasting from 60 minutes to 90 minutes - with Leader Guides featuring easy-to-follow scripts for Large Group, Small Group, and Additional Activities.

Lesson 1: Identifies the primary Bible message in the episode. A Discipleship Challenge (practical applications) is introduced to help children personalize and incorporate scriptural principles into their everyday lives throughout all three lessons of the course. 

Lesson 2: Focuses on the message as it relates to the world both historically and today.

Lesson 3: Highlights how the message ultimately comes full circle in God’s plan of sending His Son, Jesus Christ, to be our Savior.  

I already own the regular Superbook DVDs. Can I just purchase the Superbook Academy materials and not the videos?

Superbook Academy materials are not available for order separately.

No, but callers can sign up for one month and get access to all the courses.

For Annual Subscriptions- Refunds are pro-rated. 

  • For example, if the Academy curriculum was used for one month, and a refund was desired, then the remaining eleven months of the subscription will be refunded.
For Monthly Subscriptions- There are no refunds available.

How long does each lesson last?

Each lesson includes Large Group and Small Group. Additional Activities are optional.

  • Large Group (for Grades 1-6 combined): about 30 minutes.
  • Small Group (for Grades 1-3 and 4-6): about 30 minutes.
  • Additional Activities (extra games, object lessons, crafts, and Bible studies if you have extra class time): up to 30 minutes.

 

Combined, all of these provide materials for up to 90 minutes. If you have less than an hour for class, you may easily trim an activity.

What other languages are under development?

Superbook Academy is currently available in English and Spanish. Click here to see the additional languages that are currently in development: Languages in Development

Does Superbook Academy work in a homeschool setting?

Yes. The curriculum’s Leader Guides feature easy-to-follow interactive scripts that can be used with groups of children or an individual child. 

 Most games are designed for four or more children but can be adapted for fewer participants. 

Can I download Superbook Academy church curriculum?

Superbook Academy is available instantly through online streaming, rather than downloading. You can stream all of the videos, print the Leader Guides, and other resources for every course with your subscription.
