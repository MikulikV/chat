Domestic and International Relief Provided Through the Following Methods:

U.S. Hunger Strike Force

U.S. Hunger Strike Force distributes truckloads of food and drinks to local food pantries, churches, and other partner organizations. Large corporations donate food, and with the help of our donors, we send out our fleet of Hunger Strike Force semi-trucks to get that food in the hands of those who need it most. 

To find a local food pantry in the caller's area, click here. 

Famine Relief

When famine and drought strike vulnerable areas like Keyna and South Sudan, Operation Blessing rushes to the scene to provide immediate hunger relief to the suffering and malnourished. 

Agricultural & Farming 

Operation Blessing supporters supply villages and schools with agricultural training, irrigation systems, chicken coops, and farm animals to end food scarcity and hunger in parts of Latin America and Africa. Agricultural programs empower people to produce their own food by growing and raising it so that they can sell or trade food for other goods and services. 

Disaster and Crisis Relief

When a crisis or natural disasters strike, Operation Blessing provides basic needs like food, hygiene supplies, and clean drinking water. OB deploys staff, trucks a mobile kitchen, and emergency supplies to provide food in the midst of disaster.

Nutritional Support

Operation Blessing offers nutritional programs to help provide nutritious meals to children in need throughout the world and training to help parents prepare more nutritious foods for their children.

Business and Livelihood Support

Operation Blessing provides skills training and small business support for families to earn more income to consistently provide food for their children. An entrepreneurial or microenterprise program equips participants to develop small micro-sized businesses that help them earn income.

Delete

COVID-19 Relief

The COVID-19 pandemic has caused many lost jobs and incomes, making it difficult for parents to put food on the table for their children. Operation Blessing has been able to respond to these growing needs with increased food deliveries to those in need.
