General Streaming Information (All Agents)

The following section provides high-level streaming information that all agents can utilize to provide first-call resolution. 

What is Streaming?

Streaming refers to any media content – live or recorded – delivered to computers and mobile devices via the internet and played back in real-time. Podcasts, webcasts, movies, TV shows, and music videos are common forms of streaming content.

All you need to stream is a reliable and fast high-speed internet connection, access to the CBN Family App/Superbook Kids Bible App, and a compatible device.

Ways to Stream (Devices)

There are a number of streaming devices available for you to view CBN content.

Click here to learn more about the various options. 

Compatible Mobile Devices for Apps: 

• iPhone – version 5.0 and higher 

• Android – version 4.0 and higher 

• iOS – version 4.3 and higher 

Compatible Web Browsers: 

• Internet Explorer – version 9.0 and higher 

• Google Chrome 

• Microsoft Edge 

• Firefox

• Safari

 

Available Languages to Stream

CBN Family Website/App:

Streaming content on the CBN Family Website/App is available in English only, with the exception of Superbook, GizmoGO!, and Club 700 Hoy which are also available in Spanish.

However, if international users would like to read the webpage text in their language of choice, they can change their settings in Google Chrome. Note: This does not change the language spoken in videos or closed captioning. This simply will change all text that appears on the webpage. 

Superbook Kids Website/App:

Streaming content on the Superbook Website/Superbook Kids Bible App is available in all languages. 



Steps to Begin Streaming

  1. To begin streaming CBN content users must first create a free CBN.com account. Users can make a free account by visiting CBNFamily.com and clicking "Sign In" in the top right corner. 
  2. If you do not already have a CBN.com account, you can create one by selecting "Create Account".
  3. Enter your email address, password, and your name. If CBN partners, enter the email address used when you made your pledge/donation. Select “Create Account” after entering all of your information.
  4. Once you have finished creating your CBN.com account, you can sign in and enjoy streaming CBN content. 
  5. (For Partner Accounts Only) After the instant access period has ended, partners will be asked to provide their partner account number. 

Note: Once the partner has confirmed their identity by verifying their name and address information, you may give them their CBN partner account number. This is the nine-digit number in the Address Information field just above their phone number.

How Soon Can I Begin Streaming?


Free Accounts:

Users may begin streaming CBN content immediately after creating their cbn.com account. 

CBN Partners & CBN Animation Club Partners: 

CBN and CBN Animation Club partners receive instant access to streaming within about 1 hour of giving their donation. This instant access lasts for 14 days to allow the donation to be "Posted" in CRM. Once the 14 day access period has ended, the partner's access will convert to permanent streaming access. At this time, the partner may be asked to provide their partner account number to resume streaming. 

If the initial donation fails or is not received within the 14 day period, the partner's streaming access is automatically converted to the Free Account streaming access. 

If a partner misses a monthly donation, their partner streaming access will remain for 45 day from their last "Posted" donation in CRM. If a new donation is not received after the 45 day grace period, their access is automatically converted to the Free Account streaming access.  

Paid Product Orders: 

For paid product orders that offer 4K streaming like Written in Stone or I am Patrick, donors will have access to streaming within about 1 hour of giving their donation. 
 
Superbook Academy Members: 

Superbook Academy members can begin streaming their content immediately after purchase. Since Superbook Academy operates off of retail prices instead of donations, there is no grace period in the occurrence that a monthly payment is missed.

Detailed Streaming Information (DES & PCS Only) 

The following section is for streaming questions that are received by chat or are escalated to customer service. 

Streaming 1st Steps

• First: Confirm we have the current email on file in CRM and that it matches what they are using to log in 

• Be sure they are registered on the Website, check the Admin tool to confirm this 

• If they have not logged in for over 6 months, they will need to change their password, which you can help with. (this alone can save you time in helping the contact) Ask if they can log in 

• In Admin Tool, confirm they have permission to view a video or Exclusives. If they have ordered the product or are partners, you can give this permission. Check to ensure there is no stop/pause date. If so, you can change this to the following year. 

If They are Using their Phone or Computer or Laptop:

Ask if they are using the website or if have installed the CBN Family App Suggest that they clear cache, delete cookies, and update their device.

Be sure they do not have other apps open as these simple things can keep the signal from getting through. They may also need to refresh their browser or use a different browser If they have the CBN Family App downloaded, they should uninstall it before they clear cache and delete cookies then reinstall the App to be sure they have all our current updates. 

If None of This Works: 

Before making a referral, ask what is the type and model of the device they are using and include it in the referral, this way IT will have the information needed to know how to help them

Permission to Watch?

• A Contact Link and Account Link will display 

• Asset Permissions are assigned based on “Free Account” and “CBN Partner” 

• When an email address is listed under “Other Contact Links” the user has the same streaming access as the CBN Partner 

Non-Partner permissions:

• A “Contact Link” without an “Account Link” field indicates the contact is a non-CBN Partner with a registered CBN.com account 

• Asset Permissions are based on the “Free Account” content in the CBN Family Library
