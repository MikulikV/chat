Voice of the Customer Survey

Written by Darnetta Carbon | Last published at: February 02, 2023

Description 

We want to hear directly from our callers about their experience! CBN's Caller Experience Surveys are automatically sent to a portion of our callers when their email address has been verified during the call.

Our Quality Assurance and Customer Experience teams review the data to assess the areas we are excelling in and those that need adjustment.

Briefing Notes

Release Date: 2/2/23

Heads Up Announcement - Voice of the Customer Survey (All Agents)

Beginning (tentatively) on 2/6/23, the Prayer Center will launch Voice of the Customer Surveys! This is to hear directly from the caller about their experience, soon after the call. 

A portion of our calls will automatically send a 3-minute "Voice of the Customer" survey to the caller’s email, if an email address was confirmed on the call. You will not manually order any surveys for callers. 

As a reminder, it is now required to ask for or verify email addresses on every call. If a caller does not have or want to share an email address, proceed to process the call.  

If you receive a call from someone who does not want to receive or participate in surveys, process the call as follows: 

Category: Comment 

Project:  Viewer Comments 

Select: Internet Comments from the dropdown 

Referral: Digital Media Feedback  

FAQ's

Do I Order the Survey for the Caller?

No, you will not manually order the survey. Select callers will be emailed the survey shortly after the call.

What Questions Appear on the Survey?

  • What was your primary purpose for your most recent call to the CBN Prayer Center?
    • Prayer
    • Donation/Product Request
    • Account/Technical Feedback
    • I don't remember calling the CBN Prayer Center
    • Other: ____
  • On a scale of 1 to 5, please rate the following based on your recent interaction:
    • Overall Experience
    • Quickly Connecting to an Agent
    • Amount of Time Spent with the Agent
    • Request Being Fully Resolved
  • On a scale of 1 to 5, please rate the agent on the following:
    • Communication Skills
    • Willingness to Help
    • Warmth of Connection
    • Knowledgeability
  • Do you have any additional comments or feedback you would like to share on your overall experience?
